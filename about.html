About Me

Welcome to Passport Privé — where elevated travel meets personalized curation. I’m Paula, the founder behind this boutique travel brand, dedicated to designing one-of-a-kind journeys rooted in luxury, intention, and immersive exploration.


As a certified PGA golf professional, I specialize in golf travel to world-class destinations — from championship courses in Scotland to exclusive resorts worldwide. I also curate bespoke stays at five-star resorts and hand-pick cultural landmarks for travelers seeking something extraordinary.


Whether you're craving arts and culture or an indulgent all-inclusive escape, I combine insider access, supplier perks, and firsthand experience to deliver curated escapes, made effortless.
