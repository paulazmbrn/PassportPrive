About Me

Welcome to Passport Privé — a boutique travel brand where elevated journeys meet personalized curation. I’m Paula, the founder and certified PGA professional behind the brand, dedicated to crafting travel experiences that are both intentional and unforgettable.


With a unique background in golf and hospitality, I specialize in golf travel to world-class destinations — from legendary links in Scotland to hidden gems at exclusive resorts around the globe. Beyond the fairways, I curate luxury escapes that blend five-star stays, immersive cultural exploration, and thoughtful design.


Whether you’re seeking an indulgent all-inclusive or a deep dive into arts and culture, I combine insider access, exclusive perks, and firsthand expertise to create seamless, one-of-a-kind itineraries.
