Frequently Asked Questions

How do you get paid?

+

I receive a commission from hotels and partners when available. For custom itinerary design, I charge a planning fee that covers time spent researching, booking, and personalizing your trip. Planning fees are paid upfront in full before any work begins.

When should I reach out?

+

For domestic or short trips, 1-2 months ahead is ideal. For international or multi-stop trips, reach out 3-4 months in advance to get the best options and access.

What's included in your services?

+

Essentials ($200): Hotel bookings with tailored restaurant and activity suggestions for independent travelers.

Full Service ($450/week): Complete day-by-day planning including accommodations, restaurant/activity bookings, transfers, and white-glove support from arrival to checkout.

Do you help with loyalty programs and hotel perks?

+

Absolutely! Have hotel loyalty numbers? Send them my way and I'll make sure they're included so you earn points and status. I also leverage my industry connections to secure additional perks and upgrades when available.

What makes your golf travel expertise unique?

+

As a certified PGA professional, I understand golf at a deeper level than typical travel advisors. I can secure access to exclusive courses, arrange professional lessons, coordinate equipment logistics, and design itineraries that balance world-class golf with luxury accommodations and cultural experiences.

How do you ensure travel safety and handle emergencies?

+

Through my affiliation with Pinpoints Travel, you have access to 24/7 emergency support and comprehensive travel protection options. I provide detailed emergency contacts, local support numbers, and maintain communication throughout your journey to ensure peace of mind.
